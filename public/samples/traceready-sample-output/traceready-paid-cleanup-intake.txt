TraceReady paid-cleanup intake

To submit a paid cleanup order, email:
- Scope confirmation thread
- Source file or related shipment pack
- Stripe receipt email
- Commodity
- Source country
- Deadline
- Buyer summary or buyer requirements, if available

Fulfillment:
Do not pay or send raw farm coordinates before scope confirmation. TraceReady returns the cleaned ZIP pack within 24 hours after scope confirmation, payment, and receipt of a usable file. If the file is outside launch scope, TraceReady clarifies or declines the order before work begins.

Privacy:
Paid cleanup files are treated as confidential order material, kept only as long as needed for fulfillment/support/accounting, deleted on request after delivery unless retention is legally required, and not used to train AI or machine-learning models.
